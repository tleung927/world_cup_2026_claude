## Why (2–4 sentences)

The model makes this genuinely even — Opta ~52/48 to England, ~30% draw — so this is called a coin flip first, with England only a marginal lean, and that lean is taken **because the model points there and a concrete mechanism supports it**, not by overriding the number with a Messi-swansong narrative (the exact error that produced the USA–Belgium miss, applied here in reverse). That edge is offset by the other end, where Argentina's front three (Messi orchestrating from deep, Lautaro and Álvarez doing the running and finishing) attack an England defence Norway already breached twice — which is exactly why it stays a coin flip rather than a lean with conviction.
